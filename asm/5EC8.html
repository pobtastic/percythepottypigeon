5EC8: Command 08 (Or Higher): Single Tile
Used by the routine at PopulateRoomBuffer.
Command 08+: draw a single tile. The command byte itself is the tile character to draw.
Command08+_SingleTile 5EC8 LD A,(HL) A=*HL.
5EC9 INC HL Increment the room data pointer by one.
5ECA PUSH HL Stash the room data pointer and tile counter on the stack.
5ECB PUSH DE
5ECC LD D,$00 D=00 (flag: draw tile to screen buffer).
5ECE CALL DrawRoomTile Call DrawRoomTile.
5ED1 POP DE Restore the tile counter and room data pointer from the stack.
5ED2 POP HL
5ED3 DEC DE Decrease DE by one.
5ED4 LD A,D Jump back to PopulateRoomBuffer_Error if DE is zero.
5ED5 OR E
5ED6 JR Z,PopulateRoomBuffer_Error
5ED8 JP PopulateRoomBuffer_ParseByte Jump to PopulateRoomBuffer_ParseByte to continue parsing.

679C: Drop Worm
Used by the routines at MainGameLoop, Position_Worm_In_Beak and Handler_RedBird.
Drops the worm Percy is carrying.
The worm will reappear when the room is next redrawn.
DropWorm 679C LD BC,$0160 BC=0160 (number of attribute cells to search).
679F LD HL,$DE9E HL=RoomAttribute_Data.
DropWorm_SearchLoop 67A2 LD A,$1E A=1E (worm attribute value).
67A4 CP (HL) Jump to DropWorm_Found if *HL matches.
67A5 JR Z,DropWorm_Found
67A7 INC HL Advance to the next attribute cell.
67A8 DEC BC Decrease the search counter.
67A9 LD A,C Jump to DropWorm_SearchLoop if there are more cells to search.
67AA OR B
67AB JR NZ,DropWorm_SearchLoop
67AD RET Return (no worm found).
DropWorm_Found 67AE LD (HL),$00 Write 00 to *HL to remove the worm.
67B0 JR Reset_Worm_State Jump to Reset_Worm_State.

6BC8: Red Bird Direction Jump Table
Used by the routines at UpdateRedBirdMovement, 7439 and 7512.
Movement direction jump table. The entry point is self-modified at 6BC9 to jump to one of 8 directional movement handlers. Each handler adjusts B (Y) and/or C (X) by the flight speed value at *HL, then falls through to ValidateRedBirdPosition to validate the position.
